# 💡 The Problem

Customer churn represents one of the biggest challenges for companies. Identifying customers who are likely to leave before they actually do allows businesses to take preventive actions, reduce losses, and improve customer retention.

LeakHunter was developed to leverage Machine Learning and data analysis to detect customers with a high probability of churn, enabling proactive decision-making.

---

# 🤖 What is LeakHunter?

LeakHunter is a Machine Learning solution created during Hack4Her 2026 to predict customer churn through data analysis and predictive modeling.

The project focuses on transforming historical customer information into actionable insights for businesses by identifying users at risk before they leave.
